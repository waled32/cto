What makes XMR2 different?

XMR2 uses modern cryptography to hide senders, receivers, and amounts on every transaction, without giving up decentralization.

  • Every coin looks the same on-chain, preserving true fungibility.
  • Default privacy: you don’t have to remember to turn anything on.
  • Open codebase with reproducible builds and peer-reviewed research.
  • CPU-friendly mining to keep the network widely distributed.
